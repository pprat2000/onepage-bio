About Me

I have 2 years of experience as a Software Engineer in Samsung Semiconductors India Research. I was part of the Firmware team that developed the Native NVMeoF spec compliant solution using RoCE (RDMA over Converged Ethernet) as the underlying transport medium. As a result, I have in-depth knowledge of the NVMe and NVMeoF protocols.

Being from a Firmware background, I was naturally attracted to Linux. Recently, I have worked on Linux device drivers and filesystems. I have built a DMA driver for the Xilinx AXI DMA engine. This will be used by many projects in the Computer Architecture Lab @ Stony Brook University.

As a part of the Operating Systems coursework, I build a file system that automatically creates and manages backups of files when they are written to.
